Flood Projects

Direction:
Website Build-up
Layout Design

2019

In 2019, I helped Flood Projects, a film studio in Melbourne, to build up their new website. Setting up all the structures and changing the layouts made the website look more simple and clear, for more audience to use, read, and explore.

This website was built based on Squarespace, however, I also have the experience playing with other platforms including Cargo Collective, WordPress, and Wix. I also take care of the domains and hosts for the websites I buit.

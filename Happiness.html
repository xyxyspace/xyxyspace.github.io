Happiness

Film

Director

Signal
Melbourne 2020

Created as part of Signal Summer, a ten-day intensive program with VicHealth and the State Government of Victoria and City of Melbourne, responding to the provocation: "What could a Gender Equal Future look like?"

Made in collaboration with filmmakers Kate Lefoe and Hannah Moore. Screened at Signal and Bunjil Place Plaza, 2020-2022.



My Role: Director (First Time)
Conceived, directed, and edited this short in three days as part of Signal Summer with VicHealth. Working with Kate Lefoe and Hannah Moore, I learned that imperfection and laughter often go hand in hand — the film earned the loudest response at its premiere.
